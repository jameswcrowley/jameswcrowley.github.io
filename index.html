Welcome!


Driven undergraduate using computational physics to push forward our understanding of Stellar and Solar Physics.

My name is James Crowley, and I'm an undergraduate student at the University of Colorado, Boulder. Recent Projects

1. Spectropolarimetric Inversions of the Quiet Sun
I am currently working on a project at tha National Solar Observatory desinging an 'Inversion Code' using models of Solar Physics to reproduce physcal conditions of the Sun's Photosphere. Combined with data from the HINODE sattalite's Spectropolarimeter, our project aims to search for subtle differences in solar structure, notably the temperature, throughout the Solar Magnetic Cycle.
